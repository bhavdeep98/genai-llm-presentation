## Suggested Learning Order

For maximum understanding, watch in this sequence:

1. **Start here:** Andrej Karpathy — "Intro to Large Language Models" (big picture)
2. **Foundations:** 3Blue1Brown — "Attention in transformers" (visual intuition)
3. **Build it:** Andrej Karpathy — "Let's build GPT from scratch" (hands-on)
4. **Scale it:** Yannic Kilcher — Scaling Laws & Chinchilla papers
5. **Align it:** Yannic Kilcher — InstructGPT/RLHF, then DPO
6. **Efficient tuning:** Umar Jamil — LoRA explained
7. **Augment it:** LangChain — RAG from scratch series
8. **Agent it:** Yannic Kilcher — ReAct paper
9. **Alternatives:** Umar Jamil — Mamba and S4
10. **Deep dive:** Andrej Karpathy — "Deep Dive into LLMs like ChatGPT" (capstone)

## Notes

- Video links are based on well-known creators and their published content as of mid-2025. Some links may change over time — search the video title + channel name if a link breaks.
